OVERADEPT

Kyle Su

For my final project, I am going to do a continuation of my midterm project which was creating a website for my clothing brand Overadept. The brand's aesthetic is aimed to be very sleek and minimal, using very monotone colors with an apparent use of black.

I am planning to utilize bootstrap and maybe a little big of jQuery, depending what I can find useful for creating a user friendly fashion website. I am going to work off of what I had with my midterm, changing a few design aspects throughout the website. I am going to create the pages that were left unfinished, as well as add the certain aspects that polish a clothing/shopping website (UX/UI design).

As of now, I am planning to use the .carousel function a lot throughout my website, as well as the bootstrap-icons provided in bootstrap.
